A CDN allows for the quick transfer of assets needed for loading Internet content, including HTML pages, JavaScript files, stylesheets, images, and videos. Benefits; Improving website load times Reducing bandwidth costs Increasing content availability and redundancy Improving website securit At its core, a CDN is a network of servers linked together with the goal of delivering content as quickly, cheaply, reliably, and securely as possible.
